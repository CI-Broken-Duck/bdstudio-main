# Coming Soon: Reactivid

**Reactivid** is our next major release, set for 2026. It’s an AI-powered video generation platform focused specifically on **educational explainer content**. The goal is to give teachers, course creators, and curriculum developers a structured way to turn written lessons into full videos—without cameras, editors, or complex timelines.

## What It Does

At the core, Reactivid takes a script and generates a full narrated video with matching visuals. The pipeline is entirely AI-driven:

1. **Script Generation**: Users can write their own content or let our LLM generate structured lesson scripts.
2. **Text-to-Voice**: Natural-sounding voice synthesis renders narration in multiple tones and accents.
3. **SRT Output**: The system generates subtitle files aligned to spoken content.
4. **Prompt-Based Imagery**: The LLM extracts visual prompts from the script and renders static illustrations or diagrams.
5. **Timeline Assembly**: All media—voice, text, and image—is sequenced into a clean explainer format with transitions and audio timing.

This creates the effect of a **"whiteboard teacher"** or narrated slideshow—ideal for e-learning, onboarding, or async instruction.

## Who It's For

- Educators building online courses
- EdTech companies scaling curriculum
- YouTube creators and educational channels
- NGOs and nonprofits delivering instructional content in multiple languages

## Under the Hood

- **Frontend**: React-based storyboard and visual timeline interface
- **Backend**: Python, FFmpeg pipeline, FastAPI orchestrator
- **AI Stack**: GPT-derived model for script + image prompt generation, ElevenLabs or similar TTS, custom vision models for consistent image styles

## Current Status

Reactivid is currently in **private development** with the first alpha expected in Q1 2026.
