# Influent Space: AI-Powered IELTS Prep with Human Insight

**Influent Space** is our full-spectrum language testing platform, currently focused on IELTS but rapidly expanding to support additional standardized English exams. Built at the intersection of AI and education, it’s designed for students who want structure, feedback, and real guidance—without the confusion or slow progress that plagues most test prep platforms.

Whether you're targeting a Band 7+ for academic immigration, or preparing for IELTS General Training to live and work abroad, Influent Space is the tool we wish we had when we were students.

## What Makes It Different?

Influent Space brings together AI-generated feedback and private tutoring in one tightly integrated system. Our goal is to eliminate the friction between solo study and expert coaching.

Students can:
- Take full or sectional IELTS simulations (Reading, Writing, Listening, Speaking)
- Get real-time AI scoring and feedback
- Track performance with clean dashboards
- Book private sessions with certified IELTS tutors
- Practice using official and AI-generated material

Tutors can:
- Review student submissions and override AI scores
- Provide detailed human feedback using structured rubrics
- Upload custom homework and lesson plans
- Manage bookings and communications inside one interface

Everything happens in a single environment—no Zoom links, no Google Docs, no email chains.

## AI Evaluation Meets Real Pedagogy

The core of Influent Space is a custom-trained scoring engine fine-tuned on thousands of IELTS responses and examiner comments. But it doesn’t end there. Our system learns from tutor overrides and student outcomes, making it more accurate over time.

Each module includes unique AI workflows:
- **Reading**: Adaptive difficulty and time tracking
- **Listening**: Auto-synced transcripts and word recognition
- **Writing**: Band prediction, coherence analysis, grammar flagging
- **Speaking**: Pronunciation, fluency markers, and voice analysis

We believe AI is a tool—not a replacement—for real educators. That’s why every student account includes free tutor onboarding and hybrid lesson plans.

## For Who?

- Students applying for study or work visas abroad
- Working professionals needing test scores for immigration
- High school and university test prep centers
- Private tutors looking for structured student management tools

## Our Business Model

Influent Space operates on a hybrid model:
- **Free Tier**: Includes limited AI evaluations and learning resources
- **Standard Plan**: Unlocks full AI test simulations, performance dashboards, and tutor booking
- **Pro Plan**: Adds instructor-led classes, real-time session recordings, and advanced analytics
- **B2B Licensing**: Available for schools, education agencies, and language centers

## Tech Overview

- **Frontend**: React + TypeScript SPA with role-based dashboards
- **Backend**: FastAPI with PostgreSQL, scoring microservices, and Firebase authentication
- **Infra**: Dockerized deployments, Cloudflare caching, scalable API gateway
- **AI Stack**: Transformer-based essay and speech evaluators with fine-tuned rubrics

## Looking Ahead

We’re actively expanding:
- **Test Coverage**: TOEFL, Duolingo English Test, OET
- **Mobile App**: Native iOS and Android builds in development
- **Teacher Tools**: Homework banks, AI lesson suggestions, and cohort tracking
- **Live Courses**: Multi-week IELTS classes launching Q4 2025

We’re also piloting new features like:
- AI tutor matching based on learning goals
- Custom curriculum mapping by country (e.g. Canada, UK, Australia)
- Group tutoring with breakout AI review tasks
