# Introducing Tchaikovsky  
**An AI-driven music generation platform for modern creators**  
*Now live – accepting 50 beta testers*

Tchaikovsky is officially live. After months of building, training, and testing, we’re opening the gates to our first 50 beta testers. If you’re a composer, developer, or content creator looking to experiment with AI-assisted music creation, this is your chance to try it early and help shape what comes next.

## What We're Building  
Tchaikovsky is a next-generation AI music studio that turns plain English prompts into editable, multi-instrument MIDI compositions. It’s built for people who want creative control—not just AI-generated audio blobs. Whether you're scoring a short film, building a game, or drafting ideas for a new song, Tchaikovsky helps you go from concept to MIDI fast.

## Why It Exists  
Most AI music tools today fall into two camps:  
1. Generate audio you can’t really tweak  
2. Lock you into their ecosystem with little flexibility  

Traditional DAWs, meanwhile, are powerful—but getting from idea to usable music still takes hours of technical tweaking. We wanted something in the middle: a tool that understands creative intent and gets you 80% there—fast, structured, and editable.

## How It Works  
You write a prompt like:  
> “Melancholic lo-fi loop with jazz chords and ambient textures”  

Tchaikovsky responds with a complete MIDI arrangement—structured across instruments, harmonized, tempo-aware, and fully editable inside your browser. You can tweak the notes, change instruments, rearrange sections, and export it to any DAW.

## Core Features  
- **Text-to-MIDI Engine** trained on thousands of scored compositions  
- **Web Sequencer** with piano roll, mixer, and instrument controls  
- **Curated Soundfonts + VST-compatible output**  
- **Project sharing** and versioning for collaboration

## Who Should Join the Beta  
- Music producers looking for fast sketching tools  
- Game developers or film composers needing modular sound  
- Indie creators doing YouTube or podcast soundscapes  
- Music tech students and educators experimenting with AI workflows

## What You Get  
- Free access during beta  
- Priority access to new features  
- Option to request instruments, styles, and use-case support  
- Feedback sessions directly with the core team

## Business Model  
We’re running a freemium SaaS model:  
- Free tier: limited prompts and basic instrument set  
- Pro tier (launching later): higher-quality output, commercial licensing, API access

## What’s Coming Next  
- Neural audio synthesis (MIDI to rendered sound)  
- Style transfer (train on your own music)  
- Unity + Unreal plugins  
- Public prompt library
