# We’re Live: Introducing Broken Duck Media

Broken Duck Media is now officially live—and we're excited to share what we’ve been working on.

We’re a small, independent software studio based in **Chiang Mai, Thailand**, focused on building **AI-first applications** that merge storytelling, interactivity, and functional utility. Our approach is simple: build software that solves creative problems in practical, scalable ways. We design tools we want to use—and we test them with real users.

## What We Build

Our core products include:

- **[Tchaikovsky](/company/introducing-tchaikovsky)** – An AI music studio that turns natural language prompts into editable, multi-instrument MIDI compositions.
- **[Influent Space](/company/influent-space)** – A smart test-prep platform combining AI scoring and teacher input, currently focused on IELTS.
- **Reactivid** – A pipeline for generating AI explainer videos from text scripts, launching in 2026.

Each of these products is built with a lean tech stack (Python, Astro, React, FastAPI, SQLModel) and optimized for performance, clarity, and creative control. Our tools aren't toys—they're production-grade systems for creators, students, and developers who need to move fast.

## Why "Broken Duck"?

We design for edge cases, overlooked users, and creative chaos. The name **Broken Duck Media** comes from a simple idea: *a designer who could keep ducks dry in the rain*. Most software breaks under weird inputs or ambiguous creative goals. Ours shouldn’t.

## Who We Are

Broken Duck Media was founded by **Craig**, a developer and content creator with deep experience in education, media, and systems thinking. He leads product design, backend architecture, and AI workflows across all projects.

**Amber**, a co-founder and marketing strategist, brings insight into brand development, user acquisition, and content performance. She co-runs Green Light Studio with her husband **Jake**, who supports infrastructure and frontend architecture.

We're based in **Pong Noi, Chiang Mai**, just minutes from the university and surrounded by strong coffee, fast internet, and plenty of student talent.

## What’s Next

We’re actively:

- Onboarding **beta testers** for Tchaikovsky
- Improving scoring reliability in Influent Space
- Building out documentation and internal tooling
- Hiring part-time testers, writers, and AI workflow assistants

This is just the beginning. If you're a developer, educator, musician, or founder working on similar problems—or if you're curious about our tools—[reach out](mailto:admin@brokenduckmedia.com).
